## Naquadah ore
Available in Nether, Stone and Deepslate variants.
Drops raw naquadah and rarely pure naquadah.
Naturally generates on Abydos.
For more details, check [Survival Guide / Naquadah]({{ site.baseurl }}/survival/naquadah/).
